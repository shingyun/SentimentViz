      Project of Visualizing Sentiment of Fake News Articles

      Americannews.com is identified as a fake news or satire website with over 5 millions followers on Facebook. The project analyzes sentiment of articles mentioned "Trump" and "Clinton" from the website using Scrapy and TextBlob. The results show most of the articles are scored between 0.5 ~ -0.5.
